## Overview

VNX-319 maps to [CWE-319: Cleartext Transmission of Sensitive Information](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/319.html). Detects embedded cleartext HTTP/FTP URLs that suggest credentials or data transmitted without encryption.

**Severity:** High | **CWE:** [CWE-319](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/319.html) | **Languages:** go, java, node, php, python, ruby

This weakness class (Cleartext Transmission of Sensitive Information) creates a concrete exploit surface: the rule searches for the concrete source-level patterns most commonly associated with CWE-319 and surfaces them for review.
