## Remediation

1. Review each flagged line and determine whether the pattern represents a real instance of CWE-688 or a false positive.
2. Replace the flagged construct with a documented safe alternative appropriate to your language and framework.
3. For confirmed false positives, add a `# vulnetix-ignore: VNX-688` comment on the line.
4. Ensure equivalent test coverage exists to prevent regression.
