---
title: "VNX-263 – Password Aging with Long Expiration"
description: "Detect password expiration configured for one year or never."
---

## Overview

A password expiration policy of 365 days or `never` significantly extends the period a compromised credential remains usable. NIST SP 800-63 recommends shorter rotation for high-risk accounts and requires rotation on suspected compromise. This maps to [CWE-263: Password Aging with Long Expiration](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/263.html).

**Severity:** Low | **CWE:** [CWE-263](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/263.html)

## Remediation

Configure password expiry to 90-180 days for privileged accounts and enable notifications 14 days before expiry. Implement forced rotation on detected credential compromise.
