description: "Detect wildcard action grants or GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES in IAM policies and database grants."
---

## Overview

Assigning a role or privilege that allows unsafe actions violates the principle of least privilege. `GRANT ALL PRIVILEGES`, IAM `Action: *`, or roles with `permissions: ["*"]` give a compromised identity unrestricted power to read, modify, and delete resources. This maps to [CWE-267: Privilege Defined With Unsafe Actions](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/267.html).

**Severity:** Low | **CWE:** [CWE-267](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/267.html)

## Remediation

Enumerate only the specific actions and resources required. Use IAM conditions to restrict by IP, time, or MFA requirement.
