title: "VNX-268 – Privilege Chaining"
description: "Detect privileges that allow acquiring additional privileges through chaining (GRANT OPTION, role assignment)."
---

## Overview

Privilege chaining occurs when a granted privilege enables acquiring further privileges. MySQL `WITH GRANT OPTION` allows the recipient to grant the same privilege to others; a role that can call `grantRole()` lets a compromised user escalate indefinitely. This maps to [CWE-268: Privilege Chaining](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/268.html).

**Severity:** Low | **CWE:** [CWE-268](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/268.html)

## Remediation

Do not grant `WITH GRANT OPTION` to application service accounts. Restrict role assignment to administrator-only actions with strong audit logging.
