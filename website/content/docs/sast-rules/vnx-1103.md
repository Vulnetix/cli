## Overview

Detects imports of platform-specific third-party libraries that may not be available or behave differently across deployment environments. Platform-specific components can introduce supply chain risk and deployment failures in containerised or cross-platform environments.

**Severity:** Low | **CWE:** [CWE-1103](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/1103.html)

## Why This Matters

Review the flagged patterns in context. Even quality and maintainability weaknesses can have security implications when they affect how security-critical code is structured, audited, and tested.

## Remediation

1. Review all flagged instances in context.
2. Refactor code to follow the recommended patterns described in the rule.
3. Consult [CWE-1103](https://cwe.mitre.org/data/definitions/1103.html) for authoritative guidance.
