## Why This Matters

This weakness class (Placement of User into Incorrect Group) creates a concrete exploit surface: the rule searches for the concrete source-level patterns most commonly associated with CWE-842 and surfaces them for review. 2. Replace the flagged construct with a documented safe alternative appropriate to your language and framework.

4. Ensure equivalent test coverage exists to prevent regression.
