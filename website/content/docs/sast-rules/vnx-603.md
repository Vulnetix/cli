This weakness class (Use of Client-Side Authentication) creates a concrete exploit surface: the rule searches for the concrete source-level patterns most commonly associated with CWE-603 and surfaces them for review. 2. Replace the flagged construct with a documented safe alternative appropriate to your language and framework.

4. Ensure equivalent test coverage exists to prevent regression.
